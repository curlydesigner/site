About me

100% Creativity
Research, Innovation & Functionality are my principles

I grew up drawing, painting and creating. I followed my heart and completed Fine Arts and Design School, graduating with a B.Design and MA Interactive Media Design degrees.

One of my values is that I am passionate about what I do; so when I design it does not feel like I am working! The challenge I have to express myself with designs that are user friendly and functional at the same time inspiring to my clients - is what inspires and motivates me. Research, innovation and functionality are my principles, together with strong creative thinking, sense of colour and aesthetics.

Professionally I have experience in designing projects across a range of design disciplines, from initial brief to the last detail. Through working collaboratively with various clients and colleagues to finding creative solutions for customer needs. This has included design of online and offline materials, graphics, illustrations, web/ mobile UI and 3D modeling. In industries including Gaming, New Media, Entertainment, Fashion, Beauty, Exhibitions, E-commerce & Retail.

  • I believe in 'Form Follow Function'. I find both creative & functional solutions

  • A big part of what I do involves a research, customer journey, personas, latest technologies and the story behind the challenge

  • The other half I create hands on functional solutions. Designing for UI elements, websites, mobile apps, print, branding, visual representation & more

  • 3D Modelling, Video Editing & Effects
